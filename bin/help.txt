This CLI tool provides you a easy way to generate PDF from your HTML file.

Usage
  $ paper <file>
  $ paper <url>

Options
  --size,       -s  Paper size (A5, A4, A3...)
  --output,     -o  Path to export PDF
  --landscape,  -l  Landscape
  --background, -b  Print background
  --wait            Waiting time (msec) before printing

Examples
  $ paper receipt.html --size A5
  $ paper http://localhost:3000/receipt.html --landscape
